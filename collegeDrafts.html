Draft 3 - We are getting somewhere

How the story began

There was a time when I was always second, sometimes third and at other times I couldn't even achieve that. There was nothing that I could ever truly excel in. I could never be first, never be the one who stood in the front of the class as everyone clapped for me. My language skills were weak, I couldn't memorize all the different cases and exceptions in Chemistry and Biology, and History was painfully boring. Eventually I decided to give up, "Who wants to be first anyways? It's not that special" is what I thought. I had resigned myself, losing all interest in pursuing anything, and for some time I couldn't even keep up with my past scores. Fortunately, this didn't last long. In 4th grade we were introduced to our school's new computer lab and it was then that we were given our very first practical task. The teacher had asked us to draw a cool water bottle in MS Paint. Everyone wanted to draw their own desires but were failing poorly, everyone except for me. For some reason, all it took was one look and I knew exactly what to do. I had finished my task in just minutes while everyone else took an hour or more and even then, my drawing was much better than the others. For the first time ever, I was the very best. With that cam the burning question "Why stop here?".

What did I do next?

I had found my calling and now it was time to go big. I started learning how to use Microsoft Excel, Word and PowerPoint. I began HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C++ and Python; Started with Robotics, Raw Computing and Electrical Engineering as well. I just kept learning and learning, always a hundred steps ahead of everyone, but soon everyone began to catch up. I started programming and Robotics in a time when it wasn't well known by most people. In fact, nobody in my previous school, including most teachers, didn't know that Robotics actually existed. Nowadays everyone is running towards it, and the uniqueness I once had is now slowly becoming "Common". Frustration once again started to take hold and I began to question myself. What do I have to show for all those years of hard work? I have done a lot yet I have nothing decent or monumental to show.
Who am I?

Who I have become

I am Muhammad Umar Shahbaz, a 17-year old student. I am an attention seeker and try my best to be noticed. I am a people's person and get lonely quite easily. I like to make new friends and spend time with old ones, whether it's out to the movies or amusement parks, I always manage to make time. I love my parents and I help them out whenever possible. I like the feeling of independence, I prefer to buy things using my own money, I like to do my own chores or work and I like to build things on my own. Although, I still appreciate a helping hand every now and then. I know what my limits are, and I would still push against it, gaining a centimeter of progress every day, slowly but surely. I know what I can't control and am alright with it. I am a realist who always takes the third person's perspective when making important decisions, free from any arbitrary biases. I tend to see what others can't and somehow still miss the most obvious details. I am not a natural born leader but when the situation asks of me I will step up, just as I have all these past year. Even if I can't give a world class performance, I certainly can give a performance you won't forget.
